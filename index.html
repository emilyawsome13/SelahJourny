Overview

Designed to feel calm enough to return to every day.

The strongest Bible products make the main tool obvious on day one. Selah is now built around a scripture browser and a verse generator instead of stacking too many ideas into one page.

01

Book and chapter reading

Open scripture by book, choose a chapter, and keep reading inside one calm library.

02

Verse generator

Generate a verse by theme, testament, book, or chapter when you want a starting point fast.

03

Saved verse library

Keep the passages you want to return to without losing them in a generic notes app.

04

Notes and reflection

Write observations on scripture and keep your response close to what you are reading.

05

Grounded AI guidance

Use the AI guide as a study helper when you need it, without letting it replace the reading experience.

Verse generator

Four simple ways to pull a verse when you need one.

Mode 1

By topic

Generate verses around peace, hope, wisdom, love, or whatever theme you need to sit with.

Mode 2

By testament

Stay inside either the Old Testament or New Testament when you want a narrower result.

Mode 3

By book or chapter

Generate directly from the book or chapter you are already browsing so the tool stays coherent.
